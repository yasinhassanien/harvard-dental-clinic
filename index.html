About Harvard Consultant Center

A prominent dental and dermatology clinic in Qatar, established in 2017

Harvard Consultant Center is a leading dental and dermatology clinic in Qatar, established in 2017. Located in Muaither, opposite the main gate of Wathnan Mall, we are recognized for integrating advanced dental technology with a team of specialized doctors.

Our clinic features four high-tech dental operating units and emphasizes a calm, clean, and safe environment for patients. We are committed to providing high-quality care at competitive prices, making advanced dental treatments accessible and affordable for everyone in Qatar.

Comprehensive Dental & Dermatology Services

Our center covers all major dental specialties and cosmetic dermatology services, using the latest technology and techniques to ensure the best results for our patients.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive dental care for all ages

Dental Implants

Placement of titanium implants using advanced imaging (periapical, panoramic x-rays, 3D dental CT scans) for precise and efficient results.

Orthodontics

Correction of teeth alignment and bite issues using advanced appliances like Invisalign and retainers for a perfect smile.

Aesthetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, Hollywood smile makeovers, gingivectomy, and other cosmetic procedures to enhance your smile and confidence.
